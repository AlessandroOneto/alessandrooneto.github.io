Research

My research lies at the intersection of Commutative Algebra and Algebraic Geometry. I focus on developing algebraic and geometric frameworks to solve problems inspired by applied sciences, with a particular emphasis on tensor decomposition, the geometry of secant varieties, and algebraic statistics.

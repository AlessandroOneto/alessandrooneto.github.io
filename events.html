Conferences, Seminars and Events

Here is a list of my events that I have organized.

INABAG Conference 2026

Siena, Italy | 3-4 September, 2026

co-organized with Cristiano Bocci, Maria Chiara Brambilla, Emanuele Ventura

This is part of the third major event of the Italian Network for Applied and Birational Algebraic Geometry (INABAG). The event is structured into a Three-Day Summer School for graduate students and a Two-Day International Conference.

INABAG Conference 2025

Trento, Italy | 30 June - 2 July, 2025

co-organized with Elena Guardo, Elisa Postinghel, Pierpaola Santarsiero

Gathering international experts to present achievements in the theory of tensors. The conference also honored the 70th birthday of Sandro Gimigliano.

TENSORS

Poli. Torino, Italy | 10 September - 14 September, 2025

co-organized with Ada Boralevi, Enrico Carlini, Letterio Gatto, Francesco Vaccarino

Workshop bringing together experts from different branches of mathematics and engineering. Main speakers: Luca Chiantini, Wolfgang Hackbush, Bernard Mourrain, Giorgio Ottaviani and Bernd Sturmfels.

IPPI Workshop

Poli. Torino, Italy | 22 March - 24 March, 2018

co-organised with Enrico Carlini, Gianfranco Casnati, Elena Guardo, Alfio Ragusa

Following PRAGMATIC2017, this workshop helped young researchers present their work with feedback from senior researchers Cristiano Bocci and Jürgen Herzog.

Stockholm-Uppsala Mini-Conference

Stockholm University, Sweden | 12 May - 13 May, 2016

co-organised with O. Bergvall, A. Serio, and G. Sædén Ståhl

A mini-conference for PhD students to present their research in a friendly environment and network with international researchers.
